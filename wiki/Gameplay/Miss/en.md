# Miss

A **Miss** occurs when when a player doesn't interact with a hit object within its [hit window](/wiki/Beatmap/Overall_difficulty/#timing) or stops interacting during a [hit object](/wiki/Gameplay/Hit_object) that requires repeated or held input. Missing will cause the player's [combo](/wiki/Gameplay/Combo_(score_multiplier)) to break and has additional effects depending on the [game mode](/wiki/Game_mode) as shown in the table below.

## Effects of missing

| [osu!](/wiki/Game_mode/osu!) | [osu!taiko](/wiki/Game_mode/osu!taiko) | [osu!catch](/wiki/Game_mode/osu!catch) | [osu!mania](/wiki/Game_mode/osu!mania) |
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Prevent the play getting a [grade](/wiki/Gameplay/Grade) higher than an 'A' | Prevent the play getting a grade higher than an 'A' | Prevent the play getting a grade higher than an 'S' |  Prevent the play getting a grade higher than an 'S' |
